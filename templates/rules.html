Smoki Game Rules

Elements of the Game:

  • Smoki: cards from -1 to 8 (4 of each).
  • Kruki: cards 9 to 11 (4 of each).

Game Preparation:

  • The game is played with 2 players.
  • Each player receives 6 cards (only the first and the last are revealed).
  • The remaining cards are placed on three piles (two of them are revealed, and one is covered).

Gameplay:

The goal of the game is to score as few points as possible. In each turn, a player has the opportunity to peek at one of their cards. Then, the player has the following options:

  • Take a card from any face-up pile and add it to their cards (their old card is placed on the revealed pile). The player's turn ends.
  • Take a card from the face-down pile. If the taken card suits the player, they add it to their cards (their old card is placed on the face-up pile). If not, they place it on the face-up pile. The player's turn ends.
  • The player wants to end the game and check the results.

Kruki:

  • 10: If a card with the number 10 is picked up from the face-down pile, the player must take it.
  • 11: Copies the lowest value of the card lying to the right or left.

Scoring:

  • The score is the sum of the player's card values.
  • If a player has arranged two identical cards in one column, they score 0 points for them.
